How to play?

  1. Insert your desired amount of money into the deposit section.
  2. Press Begin!
  3. Choose your bet amount, by pressing the "Bet" button.
  4. Choose your line amount, by pressing the "lines" button.
  5. Press the "Spin" button to start the reels.
  6. Watch the reels spin and hope for winning combinations.
  7. If you win, your winnings will be added to your balance. You can choose to continue playing or cash out.
